=== data backup ===
--- Data backup considerations ---
> On premises
  - Tape
  - NAS
  - SAN
> Offsite
  - Cloud
> Compression
> Encryption
> Type of backup
> Virtual machine
  - Snapshots
  - Custom images
--- Full/copy backup ---
> All data is backed up
  - Longest time for backups
  - Shortest time for restore
> Incremental backup
  - New/modified data since the last inc backup
  - Shortest time to backup
  - Longest to restore
> Differential backup
  - New/modified data since last full backup
  - Quicker to restore than inc, slower than full
