=== Risk assessments and treatments ===

--- Risk assessment ---
> Prioritization of threats against assets and figuring out what to do
> Applicable to:
  - Entire org
  - Single project/department
> Targets
  - Servers
  - Legacy systems
  - IP
  - Software licensing
  
--- Risk assessment process ---
> Risk awareness
  - Cybersecurity intell sources
> Evaluate security control, current and residual
> Implement security controls
> Periodic review

--- Risk types ---
> Environmental
> Person made
> Internal
> External

--- Risk treatments ---
> Mitigation/reduction
> Transference/sharing
> Avoidance
> Acceptance
