=== Cloud deployment models ===
--- Cloud computing ---
> Running IT services on someone elses network
> Thin vs thick client
> Fog/edge computing
  - Reduce network latency
  - Storage/compute at network perimeter
--- Cloud computing characteristics ---
> Pooled resources
> Broad access
> Self-service provisioning
> Rapid elasticity
> Metered usage
-->- Public cloud ---
> Anybody can sign up
> Cloud tenant isolation
> Ongoing monthly expenses (OPEX)
> Shared IT responsibility depending on service
-->- Private cloud ---
> Cloud owned by a single org
> Requires an upfront capital investment (CAPEX)
> Org takes full responsibility
-->- Hybrid cloud ---
> Combines public and private
> Redundancy
> Disaster recovery
-->- Community cloud ---
> Government cloud
