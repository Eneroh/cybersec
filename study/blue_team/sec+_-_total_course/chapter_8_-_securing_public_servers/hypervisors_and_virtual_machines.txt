=== hypervisors and virtual machines ===
--- Hypervisors ---
> OS that manages virtual machines
> On premises (full control)
> Cloud (limited control)
> type 1
  - OS itself
> type 2
  - Runs as app on top of os
--- VM vulns ---
> Same as host hardening
  - patching
  - disable unused accounts and services
> VM sprawl
  - Unused and forgotten VMs
> VM escape
  - Attacks breaks out of VM to hypervisor
--- VM hardening ---
> Disable unnecessary components
> Encrypt VM
> Use complex passwords/MFA
> Limit public internet visibility
