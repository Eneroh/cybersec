=== Password Cracking ===

--- Hashing ---
passwords stored as hashes

--- Brute force ---
brute force attacks try character combos

--- Dictionary attack, dictionary table ---
Use lists of probable passwords

--- Rainbow table ---
Use pre-calculated hashes of passwords

--- Offline attack ---

--- Salting ---
Defeats rainbow tables (SHA-256)

--- Key stretching ---
Password based key derivation function 2 (PBKDF2)
^ Wireless network based

Salting and key stretching adds another layer of obfuscation, making passwords even harder to crack than just hasing
