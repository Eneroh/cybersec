=== public key infrastructure ===
--- x.509 ---
> Method to query systems that store certificates and also includes standards for constructing digital certificates
--- Public key cryptography standards (PKCS) ---
> details on digital certs construction and use
> Registration authority and CAs identify and authenticate individuals registering for certificates; the middle entities are called intermediate CAs, the entity at the top of the hierarchy is the root CAs
> Self-signed certs are authorized by the same entity who registers the digital certificate (not to be trusted on external connections)
