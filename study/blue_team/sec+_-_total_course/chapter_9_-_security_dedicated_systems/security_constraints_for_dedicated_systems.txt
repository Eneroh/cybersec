--- Mobile device constraints ---
--> CPU

> Battery
  - Limited power duration
> Limited transmission range
> Limited device access
  - Rooting (android)
  - Jailbreaking (apple)
--> Unable to patch firmware
  - Embedded and IoT
> Unable to change default creds
--> Mitigation
  - Replace device
  - Isolated network
